About CampusTrack

A simple and smart Student Management System designed to streamline academic operations.

Our Purpose

CampusTrack helps schools and colleges manage student information digitally—saving time and reducing manual errors.

Features

  • Manage student records
  • Track attendance
  • Course & batch details
  • Admin login system

Who Can Use?

  • School/College Admin
  • Teachers
  • Institute Staff Members

Our Mission

Our mission is to make education management faster, smarter, and digital so institutes can focus more on learning and growth.
